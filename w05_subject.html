Chocolate Cake

  • add flour, sugar, chocolate, baking powder, baking, soda, and salt to a mixer and mix until combined
  • add milk, eggs, and vanilla extract to the mixture and mix until combined
  • pour mixture into a cake pan
  • bake for 30 minutes at 350 farenheit
  • take cake out of the oven and cool for 10 minutes
  • eat the cake

Chocolate Cake

  1. add flour, sugar, chocolate, baking powder, baking, soda, and salt to a mixer and mix until combined
  2. add milk, eggs, and vanilla extract to the mixture and mix until combined; don't mix too much or else the cake will be dense
  3. pour mixture into a cake pan
  4. bake for 30 minutes at 350 farenheit
  5. take cake out of the oven and cool for 10 minutes
  6. eat the cake

Chocolate Chip Cookies

  1. whisk flour, salt, baking soda, and cornstarch together in a bowl
  2. in a different bowl, whisk melted butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar together until there are no lumps
  3. whisk in eggs and vanilla extract
  4. pour this mixture into the mixture from step 1 and mix well
  5. put the chocolate chips into the cookie dough
  6. cover the dough and leave in the fridge overnight
  7. take dough out of the fridge and let it set for 10 minutes
  8. using a cookie scoop, scoop out dough and roll each scoop into a ball
  9. place balls of dough onto baking sheets lined with parchment paper and bake for 12 minutes at 325 farenheit
  10. take cookies out of the oven and cool for 15 minutes
  11. eat cookie
